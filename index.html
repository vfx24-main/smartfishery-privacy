Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Smart Fisheries GY ("the app") is built for Guyana's fisherfolk. It is designed offline-first: your data lives on your phone, not on our servers. We operate no accounts system and no backend — we cannot see your data.

What the app stores (on your phone only)

  • Profile you enter: name, phone number, landing site, vessel and gear types.
  • Catch logs: species, weight, optional photo, optional GPS position, notes.
  • SOS records: your emergency contacts, alert history, and the GPS trail recorded during an active SOS.
  • Licence document photos and the expiry dates you confirm (read on-device; images never leave your phone).
  • Illegal-fishing (IUU) reports you compose, until they are sent.

All of the above is stored locally (app database/preferences). Deleting the app deletes this data. There is no cloud copy.

Location

The app uses your device's GPS to:

  1. suggest your nearest landing site at registration,
  2. show weather for your current position,
  3. tag catch logs (optional), and
  4. record your position trail and include coordinates in an SOS message.

Location is read only while you use these features. It is not tracked in the background and not sent to us.

Camera & photos

Used only when you choose to photograph a catch, a licence document, or evidence for an IUU report. Photos are stored on your phone.

SMS

The SOS feature pre-fills a text message in your phone's own SMS app, addressed to the emergency contacts you chose. You press send; your carrier delivers it. The app itself sends nothing on your behalf.

Internet services the app talks to

To show live conditions, the app requests data from third parties. These requests necessarily include your IP address and, for weather, the coordinates being queried:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — weather & marine forecasts
  • RainViewer (rainviewer.com) — precipitation radar tiles
  • OpenStreetMap (openstreetmap.org) — base map tiles
  • Google Fonts (fonts.gstatic.com) — typefaces, cached after first load

No account identifiers are sent to these services. The app contains no ads, no analytics, and no trackers.

Data sharing

We share nothing, because nothing reaches us. Data leaves your phone only when you act: sending an SOS SMS, or submitting an IUU report through the app's queue (relayed to the Fisheries Department's contact channels).

Children

The app is intended for working fisherfolk and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We will update this page if the app's data practices change (for example, if a shared leaderboard service is added, which would involve an account and a server — any such change will be clearly announced in the app).
